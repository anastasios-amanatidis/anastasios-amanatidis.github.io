About

As a Freelance Data Scientist, I work on the preparation, processing, and analysis of data using Python and SQL, as well as on process automation and the development of data-driven solutions that turn complex, manual workflows into efficient and scalable systems. As a Data Analyst and Data Engineer, I design and implement ETL pipelines and build dashboards in Power BI that deliver insights that are actionable rather than just informative.

I hold a Master's degree in Mathematics with a specialization in Data Science and Algorithmic Engineering, which gave me a strong analytical foundation and a structured approach to solving data problems.

My background is in actuarial analysis, where I work primarily in company pension schemes (bAV). These experiences confirm what my studies had already pointed toward: I work best at the intersection of mathematics, data, and process improvement.

I am currently expanding my skill set into PySpark and Machine Learning — areas increasingly relevant for scalable, AI-driven data solutions. My objective is to help organizations move beyond manual processes and build data infrastructure that is reliable, efficient, and built to scale.
